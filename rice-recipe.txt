Ingredients:
 - 2 cups rice
 - 4 cups water
 - 2 teaspoons salt
 - 2 teaspoons vegetable oil


Directions:
 1. In a pot, pour the rice. Then add the water, salt and vegetable oil and mix.
 
 2. Place the pot, with open lid, on the cooker. Put on a medium flame and wait until the water start to boil.
 
 3. Lower the flame and put on the lid, leaving about a quarter of the pot oncovered.

 4. Mix every 5-10 minutes for 20 minutes.

 5. Voila
